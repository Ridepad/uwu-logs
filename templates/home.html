Welcome to UwU Logs

UwU Logs is a World of Warcraft combat log parser.

Supports any Wrath of the Lich King (3.3.5) server.

New feature - Perfomance Points

Features include

  • Damage top
  • Detailed damage/heal/damage taken breakdown
  • Actions timeline
  • Useful damage
  • Consumables
  • Deaths
  • Valk grabs
  • Player auras
  • Comparison between same classes
  • PvE Stats

SoonTM

  • All fights history by player
  • Logs search (filter forms - guid, spell, etc.)
  • Friendly fire: Bloodbolt Splash, UCM, bpc vortex
  • Self harm: UCM, Chilled to the Bone
  • Top: no externals
